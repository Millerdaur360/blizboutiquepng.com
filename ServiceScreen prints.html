    SCREEN PRINTING

    What is Screen Printing?

    Screen printing is a versatile printing technique used to transfer images or designs onto various surfaces, such as textiles, paper, plastics, and more. It is a popular method for producing high-quality prints in large quantities, making it widely used in the production of promotional items, apparel, signage, and other materials.

    Advantages of Screen Printing

    • Versatility: Ability to print on various materials

    • Vibrant and long-lasting colors

    • Precision in reproducing intricate designs
